Our Story

Experience the authentic flavors of India at Madras Darbar Restaurant, a renowned culinary destination in Thailand. With a rich 18-year legacy, we've grown from a humble beginning to three thriving locations, serving the finest non-vegetarian South and North Indian cuisine.

Our Heritage

At Madras Darbar, we're passionate about sharing India's rich culinary heritage with Thailand. Our journey began in 2006 with a simple mission: to create authentic Indian flavors using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients.

Our master chefs bring decades of experience from different regions of India, ensuring every dish reflects the authentic tastes and aromas that have been cherished for generations.

Dine Out With Us

Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that will transport you to the vibrant streets of India. With a commitment to quality, flavor, and hospitality, Madras Darbar Restaurant is the perfect destination for anyone seeking authentic Indian cuisine in Thailand.
